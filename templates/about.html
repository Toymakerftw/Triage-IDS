About Triage

Triage is a powerful Network Intrusion Detection System (NIDS) designed to analyze network traffic and detect malicious nodes and anomalies within pcap files.

Using advanced machine learning algorithms, including XGBoost and Isolation Forest, Triage employs intelligent techniques to identify potential threats and suspicious activities within network communication.

With its user-friendly interface, Triage provides detailed insights into network traffic patterns, highlighting malicious IP addresses and offering packet-level details for further investigation.

Moreover, Triage goes beyond detection by integrating with iptables, allowing administrators to block suspicious or malicious devices from accessing the network, providing an additional layer of defense.

Whether you are a network administrator, security analyst, or IT professional, Triage offers a comprehensive solution to enhance network security, detect threats, and safeguard your infrastructure.
